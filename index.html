The Brief

Strategic Communications for Primary Health Care (PHC)

Core Mandate: Develop an actionable communications plan that drives improved access, quality, and uptake of PHC services—especially for women and children—by translating strategy into bold, locally relevant storytelling.

Strategic Goals

  • • Build public trust and drive behavioral change
  • • Improve donor awareness and funding
  • • Strengthen community engagement via local champions

The Challenge

The Gates Foundation needed to bridge two worlds: speaking to policymakers with data-driven evidence while reaching rural communities with culturally authentic narratives that drive trust.
