The Brief

Strategic Communications Campaign for Primary Health Care (PHC)

Core Mandate: Develop an actionable communications plan that drives improved access, quality, and uptake of PHC services—especially for women and children—by translating strategy into bold, locally relevant storytelling and execution.

Strategic Goals

  • • Build public trust and drive behavioral change
  • • Improve donor awareness and funding
  • • Strengthen community engagement via local champions

The Challenge

The Gates Foundation faced a dual-audience communications challenge: speaking to policymakers in a data-driven ROI language, while reaching rural communities through culturally authentic storytelling.

Complexity Factors:

  • • Systemic mistrust of public health
  • • Cultural resistance to family planning
  • • Misinformation within local networks
  • • Low national visibility of PHC

A visual synthesis of the "My Healthy Nigeria" movement, bridging systemic health infrastructure with personal human stories.

Creative Approach

"My Healthy Nigeria, Starts With PHC."

The campaign united donors and citizens around one truth: real change starts with trusted, local primary care. We tied personal hopes for well-being to a collective solution for a stronger nation.

Strategic Pillars

Mass Entertainment
Storytelling to normalize health behavior.
Policy & Influence
Narratives to shape donor agendas.
Celebrity Engagement
Trusted voices humanizing PHC.
Community Activation
Grassroots activation with local voices.
